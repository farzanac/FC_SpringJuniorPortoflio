Jason's Rocky Island

Jason's Rocky Island is one of my first 3D Unity game that I created Sophmore year for the G4C competition. The theme was Environment. The Game is about: To save your village from starvation, you must sail to an island and collect coins while evading "Jason the magnet," a tool controlled by a hostile neighboring village planning an invasion. You play as a rolling ball who collects the coins while avoiding rocky obstacles and Jason the magnet who is trying to take the coins for himself
