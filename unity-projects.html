UNITY PROJECTS

These are my favorite projects that I made from Unity

Jason's Rocky Island

Jason's Rocky Island is one of my first 3D Unity game that I created Sophmore year for the G4C competition. The theme was Environment. The Game is about: To save your village from starvation, you must sail to an island and collect coins while evading "Jason the magnet," a tool controlled by a hostile neighboring village planning an invasion. You play as a rolling ball who collects the coins while avoiding rocky obstacles and Jason the magnet who is trying to take the coins for himself.

Link To G4C Website

Space Blaster

For this project, I created a 2D shooter game in Unity where the player controls a character that moves around the screen and shoots enemies. The goal of the game is to destroy enemies while staying alive. The theme of this game is On the Road opposing the idea of the sky being your limit. Players get to travel in space following traffic laws while avoding collusion with nearby spaceships.

Green Man in Rainy Forest

This project is also related to G4C however the theme is Reef and Rainforest. THis game addresses food scarcity and balance in the environment by showing how limited resources like melons can create competition between humans and animals. It teaches the player with the concept of the player being the invasive species or human impact, the melons representing natural resources like food and plants and the hippos as the native rainforest animals trying to survive. As the player collects more resources, the environment becomes damaged and animals struggle to survive.
